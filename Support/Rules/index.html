SuperBot Support Server - Rules

Please check this page for in-depth information about the rules.

Server rules

  1. No invisible or “inappropriate” Nicknames.
  2. No “inappropriate” avatars, profile pictures, usernames, nicknames, statuses, banners, linked accounts and about-me's.
  3. No impersonation.
  4. No unnecessary Direct Messaging.
  5. No "inappropriate" content. This includes but is not limited to text messages, files, links, commands, voice input (typically microphone) and video input (typically camera).

Text Channel Rules

  1. Avoid overusing bot commands outside the channel.
  2. Do not ask for roles, it will make you less likely to get them. (Exceptions are News, Downtimes and custom colour roles.)

Voice Channel Rules

  1. No annoying, loud or high-pitched voices.
  2. Avoid excessive pranking or earrape.

Inappropriate items:

  • Scamming, hacking or otherwise illegal content
  • Pornographic Content or Dating
  • Harassment, racism, swearing, hate speech or otherwise offensive content
  • Spamming or excessive advertising
  • Excessive political or religious content
  • Excessive negative comments on the bot. (We accept constructive ideas)
  • Promotion of any content listed above

Depending on the severity, you might be kicked, muted or banned from the server, temporarily or permanently.
