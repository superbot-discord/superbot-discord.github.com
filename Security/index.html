Privacy Policy

Last updated 27 Dec 2021 UTC
We value everything about you — your experience, your feedback — but most importantly, your privacy.

Bot

We ONLY store information listed below across the entire bot:

  • Arguments : All command arguments are temporarily stored.
  • Snipe : All deleted messages' contents are temporarily stored. You can disable access to this piece of information in your server by disabling snipe (=snipe disable). You can clear all sniping data in your server by running =clearsnipe.
  • Poll : Information about polls initiated by =poll is stored.

Cookies

All cookies we store do not come directly from you. We ONLY store the cookies listed below across the entire site:

  • Access Token (as ACT): A token gotten from Discord required to interact with their APIs
  • Refresh Token (as RST): A token gotten from Discord required to renew your Access Token when it times out

Localstorage

Localstorage can only be read by you, not our servers. We ONLY store the localstorage fields listed below across the entire site:

  • Background Color (as b): Background color of the website
  • Foreground Color (as f): Foreground color of the website
  • Dark mode? (as dark): Whether dark mode is turned on for the website or not

Information we collect

We never use web analytics services or other means to collect any information from you other than those listed above. All information collected is only used to provide the service of display the website properly. We do not have access to your information.
